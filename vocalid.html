VocaliD

Branding

VocaliD is an innovative Biotech startup founded by Rupal Patel that builds custom synthetic voices. If you're familiar with Stephen Hawking's iconic voice, you may be shocked to learn that many people around the world - young and old, male and female - speak with the exact same voice. There are tens of millions of people worldwide who rely on computerized voices to express themselves through text-to-speech technology.

I designed their logo, created their brand, and directed brand usage through both the website and immersive recording platform for speech donation. This work has been featured at leading events such as TEDMed, Business Innovation Factory and national outlets like Science Friday.

The shape of the logo is derived from the physical anatomy of vocal chords, layered with a lined texture to create a sense of vibration. While it's a fairly simply logo, it is evocative of everything VocaliD stands for: boldness, voice, and technical detail.

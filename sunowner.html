Sunowner

Branding

Sunowner is a renewable energy startup that is creating a community of solar and energy efficiency advocates, with the goal of bringing them all to a Net-Zero energy status. They will do this by leveraging smaller steps users can make to reduce their energy bills and carbon footprint, such as LED lighting and smart thermostats.

All targeted toward the solar energy user or advocate, Sunowner is one part a community-building network, one part a marketplace for energy saving devices, and one part a technology for measuring energy use. The branding has to work for multiple use cases, be relevant in the tech space, and also communicate sun and solar. To do so, we kept it simple and versatile. Utilizing circles to show continuity and emulate the sun, we doubled the shape and added a warm gradient. It's square so it can work for multiple use cases varying from an app icon to a brand identity.

We developed the rest of the brand off of the logo and created multiple brand elements, manipulating the logo to add more dimensionality to the brand.

Mobile Application

Sunowner partnered with a UK energy metering company to bring their technology to the USA. The app needed to be redesigned to be more user friendly, so we re-did the entire project.
